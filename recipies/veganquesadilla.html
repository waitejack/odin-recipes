Vegan Quesadilla

Description

If you are looking for a super simple vegan dinner idea, you've come to the right place!

The average quesadilla is mostly cheese and meat. Very much not vegan. I've cooked these for my vegan girlfriend and we both loved them.

Ingredients

  • Small tortillas
  • Shredded vegan cheese
  • Yves Mexican Veggie Ground
  • Bell pepper
  • Sweet onion
  • Minced garlic
  • Taco seasoning
  • Cooking oil
  • Optional: Salsa and vegan sour cream to dip!

Steps

  1. To start, slice and sautee your onions and peppers in a small amount of oil.
  2. As the onion and pepper gets close to done, add minced garlic and a bit of taco seasoning.
  3. Once the veggies are done to your liking, mix in the Yves Mexican Ground and heat through. Set aside.
  4. Heat a small amount of oil in the pan.
  5. Lay down a tortilla and assemble the quesadilla. Layer of cheese, layer of veggie/"meat" mix, layer of cheese again, then the top tortilla.
  6. Squish down gently to ensure everything melts together and cook until the bottom tortilla is browned.
  7. Carefully flip and cook until browned again.
  8. Enjoy!
